Who are we?

Qualified and professional behavioural consultant that aims to provide world-class services to support children with emotional and behavioural difficulties as well as children with autism in living a fulfilled and independent lives.

Why pick us?

The behavioural consultant is a passionate individual who is currently in the process of receiving BCBA certification. She aims to bring the best standards of intensive behavioural treatment for children with behavioural difficulties and children diagnosed with autism across various setting (e.g. clinic, home and school).

What we do and what will our services include?

Your child will receive a comprehensive assessment (Verbal Behaviour Milestones Assessment and Placement Program VBMAPP) at the onset service. This assessment involves identifying the skills that your child needs to learn (e.g., language, social skills, self-help skills) and then designing an individual curriculum to teach those skills. Your child will receive this complimentary assessment every 9 months to monitor their skill development. Curriculum will be reviewed weekly in a team meeting and new programmes will be added according to the child’s progress.
